The Beginning

From Achievement to Awakening

Reclaim didn't start as a media company. It started with a breakdown.

After years of chasing every marker of success, the career, the accolades, the recognition, Rudi Ribeiro found himself at the top of a mountain he never wanted to climb. Everything looked right on paper. Everything felt wrong on the inside.

"I achieved everything I thought I wanted and realized I had been running from myself the whole time."

That moment of painful clarity became the seed of Reclaim. Not another self-help brand promising optimization. Something different: a space for men to ask the real questions.

Who am I when I stop performing? What do I actually want? What was I made for?

Meet the Founder

Rudi Ribeiro

Founder & Chief Storyteller

Rudi is a former high-performer turned purpose advocate. After a decade in the corporate world, he walked away from a successful career to pursue the question that haunted him: "Is this really it?"

What he discovered changed everything. The validation he had chased his whole life was never going to fill the void, because the void wasn't about achievement. It was about identity.

Now Rudi dedicates his life to helping other men make that same discovery before they waste years climbing the wrong mountain. Through Reclaim, he's built a platform, podcast, and community that reaches millions of men worldwide.

When he's not creating content, you'll find him with his family, in nature, or having deep conversations with men who are ready to do the real work.
