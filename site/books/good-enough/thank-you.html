A note from Rudi

I wrote this book because I lived it. For years, perfectionism ran my life. It looked like ambition from the outside, but inside it was exhaustion, anxiety, and never feeling like I was doing enough.

This book is the conversation I wish someone had with me years ago. I hope it helps you breathe a little easier and finally give yourself permission to be good enough.
