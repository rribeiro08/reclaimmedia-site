Tips for Playing Colby Hayes Songs

  • Fingerpicking: Many songs use a Travis picking pattern. Practice the bass note alternation with your thumb.
  • Dynamics: Build intensity gradually through verses into choruses. Pull back during bridges.
  • Strumming: Keep it loose and emotional. These aren't rigid patterns but conversations.
  • Capo Use: Most songs use a capo. The key shown is the sounding key, not the chord shapes.
