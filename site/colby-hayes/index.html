About Colby Hayes

Colby Hayes is an indie-pop artist making music for anyone who's ever stayed up too late overthinking. With a sound that blends the emotional vulnerability of Conan Gray with the soulful depth of Hozier, his songs capture the messy reality of growing up, falling out of love, and finding yourself somewhere in between.

From bedroom recordings to late-night confessions, "Static in the Dark" is the soundtrack to every 3AM thought you've ever had. Whether you're nursing a broken heart or just trying to figure out your twenties, there's a song here that gets it.

Colby Hayes is an AI-powered music project exploring the intersection of technology and authentic indie songwriting.
