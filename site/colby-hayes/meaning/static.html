What Is "Static" About?

"Static" is the title track and emotional centerpiece of the album. It uses radio frequency as a metaphor for connection: you used to come in crystal clear, now all that's left is white noise.

The song captures that gradual erosion of communication in a relationship. When you used to talk for hours about nothing, and now you can barely find words. When every attempt to tune back in just brings more interference.

Key Themes

  • Lost Connection: The frequency you used to share
  • Effort vs. Result: Tired of searching through the static
  • Impermanence: Some connections just fade
  • Acceptance: Some signals never mend
