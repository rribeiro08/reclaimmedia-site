About Reclaim Media

Reclaim Media is an AI-powered creative studio producing music, content, and digital experiences. Founded in 2026, we explore the intersection of artificial intelligence and authentic creative expression, creating AI artists with real stories to tell.

Our debut artist, Dakota Rose, is a country music project that has gained attention for its heartfelt songwriting and powerful vocals. The album "Heart Wide Open" features 12 original tracks available on all major streaming platforms.

Key Points:

  • AI-powered creative studio based in the United States
  • Focus on authentic storytelling through technology
  • Transparent about AI creation process
  • Debut artist Dakota Rose with full-length album
  • Available for brand partnerships and licensing
