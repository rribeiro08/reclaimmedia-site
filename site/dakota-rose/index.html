About Dakota Rose

Dakota Rose is an AI-powered country music project blending heartfelt storytelling with powerful vocals. From the quiet ache of "3 AM" to the fiery passion of "Wildfire Love," every song captures the real moments that make up life in a small town.

Based in Nashville, Tennessee, Dakota Rose brings together classic country roots with a modern sound. Whether you're driving down a red dirt road or dancing under string lights on a Saturday night, there's a song here for you.

Dakota Rose is an experimental AI music project exploring the intersection of technology and authentic country songwriting.
